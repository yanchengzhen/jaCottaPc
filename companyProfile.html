Shanxi Jiakun International Trading Co, Ltd.

Jacotta is specially engaged in the ceramic dinnerware and drinkware for several years,which is located in Taiyuan city,Shanxi Province,China. Our competitive products are plates,bowls,mugs (advertising mug & promotional mug), cup and saucer,dinnerware sets,etc.which made in porcelain, stoneware, new bone china and fine bone china material, All the products have passed SO9001, SDA, SGS, 84 / 500 / EEC, LFGB Certification,etc.We have exported more than 40 countries and areas,including North America,South America,Europe,Australia,Middle East and Southeast Asia.

Adhering the market as the center, Responding rapidly to the market dynamics, keeping create long-term value for customers. Provide effective service to customers is our direction and the value of evaluation of the scale. Customer’ success is our success.
